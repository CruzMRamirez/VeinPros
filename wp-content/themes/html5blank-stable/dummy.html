What are Hard Varicose Veins and Spider Veins?

Varicose and spider veins are symptoms of a problem called superficial venous reflux. This occurs when, in veins near the skin on the legs or face, the valves that prevent the backflow (reflux) of blood fail or become defective. The defective valves create poor circulation in the veins, allowing blood to backflow and pool, bulging and deforming the veins. The result is either a spiderweb of blue lines under the skin (spider veins), or twisted and protruding knots (varicose veins).

For most, the appearance of hard varicose veins or brightly colored spider veins raises only a cosmetic concern. But in some cases, the underlying cause of poor vein circulation can create additional complications. Here are some of the potential symptoms:

  • The appearance of red, blue, or purple veins underneath the skin
  • The appearance of twisted, bulging, or otherwise misshapen veins
  • An ache or heavy feeling in the legs
  • Sensations of throbbing, cramping, burning, or swelling in the legs
  • Itching around the veins

In severe cases, the following symptoms can develop:

  • Pain and discoloration of the skin in the affected area
  • Bleeding from affected veins
  • Hardening of the vein
  • Skin ulcers, which can lead to infection

Treatments

Those who suffer from varicose or spider veins can mitigate some of the symptoms by using compression stockings to promote blood flow, and getting active. This eases the condition, but does not resolve it. Undoing the damage done by superficial venous reflux requires closing the defective veins so the body can rebuild.

This can be done in a number of ways, ranging from shots to surgically removing the veins. At VeinPros in Fresno, CA, we use a more sophisticated approach. It’s called the ClosureFast procedure, and it uses radiofrequency ablation to heat up the problematic vein, causing it to close and eventually fade away. The body safely reroutes blood to healthy veins; recovery is quick, and pain is minimal with this outpatient procedure. Not only does this relieve the cosmetic symptoms of varicose and spider veins, but it also resolves the underlying cause of more extreme symptoms.
